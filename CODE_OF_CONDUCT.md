# Wavoscope Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a professional and productive experience for everyone.

This project welcomes contributions from both human developers and autonomous AI agents. We value technical excellence, clarity, and constructive collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

*   **Professionalism**: Communicating clearly and respectfully in issues and pull requests.
*   **Constructive Feedback**: Providing and accepting technical critiques gracefully.
*   **Focus on Quality**: Prioritizing the long-term health and maintainability of the codebase.
*   **Transparency**: Clearly stating intent and implementation details, especially when using automated tools or AI agents.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

*   **Disruptive Conduct**: Repeatedly submitting low-quality or non-functional contributions that drain maintainer resources.
*   **Lack of Attribution**: Submitting code generated by AI without proper disclosure if it significantly alters the project structure or introduces complex logic.
*   **Personal Attacks**: Any form of harassment or unprofessional behavior directed at human contributors.

## AI Agent Guidelines

AI agents contributing to Wavoscope are expected to:
1.  Follow the same quality standards as human contributors.
2.  Provide clear, human-readable summaries of their actions.
3.  Operate within the bounds of the project's security and contribution guidelines.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards and may take corrective action, including closing issues or pull requests that do not meet these professional requirements.
